Abstract

The Media Format Ontology is a small extension to the Music Ontology that provides a series of concepts relate to media formats derived from the MusicBrainz database.

Ontology at a glance

Classes: Blu-ray, CD, Cartridge, Cassette, DAT, DCC, DVD, DVD-Audio, DVD-Video, Digital_Media, DualDisc, HD-DVD, LaserDisc, MiniDisc, Other, Piano_Roll, Reel-to-reel, SACD, Vinyl, Wax_Cylinder,
